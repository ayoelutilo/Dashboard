🤝 Problem Statement

There are so many organization apps available for us to choose from Todoist to Trello to Notion; So many awesome apps but having these many apps with different features and updates can sometimes be overwhelming. And one thing we try to make sure with organization is consistency. Most people find it to be consistent especially if they're expected to be consistent with different apps. It can sometimes get hard. The other option for them is to simplify their process by assigning one thing to another but then if they use apps like notion or apple notes to document their thoughts and lists they can sometimes loose focus because those are not the traditional apps for such features.

Hub solves all these issues, How?

The app synchronizes all the organization apps you use and with the help of an AI buddy, Losi, You can maintain consistency in the things that matter. This not just an organization app but a Dashboard for your life. The ultimate productivity hack.

Synchronize and even create your very own

  • Todo List
  • Planner
  • Calendar
  • Life Timeline
  • Board
  • Journal, and so on...

Pain

Summary of the high level pain, followed by the specific pain points below.

Customers find it hard to to maintain consistency especially for a long period of time .

  • Users have multiple apps for different ways or organizing. The combination of these apps can be very overwhelming and most times can go against the aim of organization

    Impact: The users now have to go from one app to the other depending on the requirement they have in regards to organization

    Mitigation: Users try to have as little apps as possible for organization or they put them in a folder. Some times, since we have a lot of apps, these apps can just get lost on the home screen.

    Some users try to avoid it by having one app for one major organizational focus

    Signal: Users are still on a journey to reduces being overwhelmed especially in this age where most things are cloud based and can be done from anywhere.

Context

Hub is basically an integration node that can easily migrate your lists or means of organization from other apps and bring them into one app. This app can then sync with all your devices to bring all the things you need organized in one place.

It's an AI enabled Dashboard that simplifies the complexity of certain organizational tasks or skills down to either pins on your dashboard or skills or power-ups for pins

Hub is the technology equivalent to when the phone was made. Before it, there were different tools we would need to carry around like a clock, calendar, calculator, measuring tape and so on, These tools were not necessarily due for an upgrade when they were moved on to the phone. But the reason for the iPhone was to put all these things in one place. The strength of this transition was synchrony. The clock on the iphone told the same time as the clock on your desk. The calender on your iphone told the same date as that on your wall. This synchrony was what made the migration and ease of use of iPhones smooth.
