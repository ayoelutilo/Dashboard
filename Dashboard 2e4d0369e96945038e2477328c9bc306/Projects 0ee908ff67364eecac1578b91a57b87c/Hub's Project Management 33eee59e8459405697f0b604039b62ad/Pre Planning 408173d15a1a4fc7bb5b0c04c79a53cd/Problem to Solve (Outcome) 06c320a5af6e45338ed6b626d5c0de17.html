Problem to Solve (Outcome)

According to Wikipedia, a problem to solve or a problem statement is a concise description of an issue to be addressed or a condition to be improved upon. It identifies the gap between the current (problem) state and desired (goal) state of a process or product.

How to write an effective Problem Statement - iSixSigma

  • What is the problem that needs to be solved?

    Inability to focus or keep consistency in organization because of the fast pace of technology and innovation without a central synchronized system or database.

  • Why is it a problem? (highlight the pain)

    There are so many organization apps available for us to choose from Todoist to Trello to Notion; So many awesome apps but having these many apps with different features and updates can sometimes be overwhelming.

  • Where is the problem observed? (location, products)

    On organization apps or when organization apps have to be combined.

  • Who is impacted? (customers, businesses, departments)

    Freelancers and people that run their businesses or organization are impacted the most.

  • When was the problem first observed?

    When it was first observed personally was when i started using more that one organization app. Combining Trello, Notion, Monday and Todoist supercharged my productivity in a way that was unfound. And the cool thing about all of them is that they have a very strong community behind them so they all have intense strengths that are usuually unique to them. so because of that people tat wasnt their productiviy supercharged are forced to download each of these individual apps. That's where hub comes in. Bringing all these intense, huge and incredibly creative communities into one dashboard gives all of them the ability to supercharge their productivity in a way that has never been seen before.

  • How is the problem observed? (symptoms)

    Forgetting tasks that have been set in an app that wasn't opened that day.

  • How often is the problem observed? (error rate, magnitude, trend)

    They start getting overwhelmed between 2 weeks - a month.
